UC Berkeley Computer Science 194-017: Programming the Cloud

"Programming the Cloud" is an undergraduate seminar offered at Berkeley by Joe Hellerstein and Peter Alvaro, Fall 2011.

The course explores distributed systems fundamentals as well as new challenges in cloud computing, through a practical lens of software development. As part of the course, students undertake significant software development in a language for distributed systems like Bloom or Erlang.
